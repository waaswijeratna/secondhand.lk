Stay Safe

At SecondHand.lk, we are 100% committed to ensuring that your experience on our platform is as safe as possible. Here's some advice on how to stay safe while using SecondHand.lk.

Our general safety guidance

    Keep it local! Meet the seller in person and inspect the item before making any payment.

    Never share financial information. This includes bank account details or any other details that could be misused.

    Apply common sense. Steer clear of anything that seems too good to be true, like unrealistically low prices or promises of quick money.

    Stay vigilant for scams and frauds. Avoid sharing personal information and steer clear of transactions that seem suspicious.
