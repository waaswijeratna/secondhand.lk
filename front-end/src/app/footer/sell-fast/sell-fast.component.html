Sell Fast

Here are some tips on how to create ads that capture buyer interest.

Include as much detail as possible. Ads with clear information attract more views! Use keywords and share details that buyers find interesting. Always be honest in your descriptions.

Increase your ad's appeal by adding great photos. Utilize clear images of the item for sale, as ads with real photos can attract up to 10 times more views than those with catalog or stock photos. Ensure good lighting and capture photos from various angles.

Choose the right price for your item. Find inspiration from similar ads on secondhand.lk. Generally, a competitive price boosts demand. Opt for the 'Negotiable' option if open to bargaining. Remember, the right price can make all the difference!

Boost your ad's visibility with Ad Promotions! After creating your excellent ad, apply Ad Promotions to attract more views and interested buyers. Higher demand increases your chances of selling quickly at your desired price.
