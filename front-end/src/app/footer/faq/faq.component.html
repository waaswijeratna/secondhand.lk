How do I post an advertisement?

Listing your item on SecondHand.lk is both cost-free and straightforward! Just tap the 'POST AN AD' button and proceed with these steps,

  • Click on the 'Post Ad' button on the homepage.
  • If you are a new user, register on SecondHand.lk by entering your details.
  • If you are already a registered user, enter your email and password and submit.
  • Enter your ad details, upload photos, and click submit.

Your advertisement will be published once it has been approved by our team. This process typically takes only 15 to 30 minutes. Start selling on SecondHand.lk and connect with our community today!
